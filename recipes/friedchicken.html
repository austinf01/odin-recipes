Oven Fried Chicken

Description

Making traditional fried chicken can be unhealthy; however, by cutting down on oil and cooking in the oven it can make you feel better about eating this delicious recipe

Ingredients

  • 12 Chicken thighs, breasts, legs, wings, or drumsticks
  • Flour
  • Bread Crumbs
  • Eggs
  • Seasonings
  • Oil

Directions

  1. Dredge the chicken thighs by coating them in the flour (seasoned with salt and pepper)
  2. Coat in eggs and Bread Crumbs
  3. Pour a generous splash of oil into a baking dish and put the chicken inside
  4. Sprinkle with Paprikra and bake in a preheated oven for 30 minutes
  5. Flip the chicken and bake in a preheated oven for another 30 minutes
  6. Drain the cooked chicken on paper towels before serving
